La Playa Dayclub Unveils Thanksgiving Week Plans and Opens Guest List for Locals

VIP Table At La Playa

Photo by D. Joseph Photography

Fort Lauderdale, FL - Thanksgiving week the hottest new House Music Beach Club in Fort Lauderdale, La Playa Dayclub, invites you for a celebration.

During this inaugural Thanksgiving week, both locals and tourists 21 and over can enjoy a special Wednesday evening experience, extended Friday Hours, a full La Playa Dayclub Vibes Saturday featuring our favorite resident DJs, and an exclusive, ticketed Sunday Funday Takeover in partnership with House Hats Collective, a community of creatives founded in 2019 with the mission of growing artists through creatively, self-expression, and wellness.

We have been looking forward to this week for a long time, says Brando Garcia-Rovai, General Manager of La Playa Dayclub.  We have a great staff, incredible DJs, gorgeous beach views, and a great vibe. It sets the tone for a perfect South Florida Thanksgiving adult break!

Sticking with the motto La Playa Loves Our Locals, La Playa Dayclub is offering free admission on Wednesday, November 23, Friday, November 25, and Saturday, November 26 to anyone on our guest list.  Sunday will have a limited number of free RSVP tickets and will charge a cover of $10 at the door.  In addition, to give back to our friends and family, La Playa Dayclub will be hosting contests on their Instagram and Facebook for those 21+ to win a free VIP experience which includes a VIP Cabana and one free bottle served tableside.

Hours Of Operations

Wednesday, November 23, 2022, 4 pm -10 pm - Get On The Guestlist

Thursday, November 24, 2022 - (CLOSED FOR THANKSGIVING)

Friday, November 25, 2022, 11 am - 10 pm - Get On The Guestlist

Saturday, November 26, 2022, 11 am - 10 pm - Get On The Guestlist

Sunday, November 27, 2022, 11 am - 10 pm (Special Ticketed Event)

About La Playa Dayclub

Awaken your sense of discovery as you step into the intimate jungle oasis of La Playa Dayclub, Fort Lauderdale Beachs first true luxury day club experience. Nestled on the 2nd floor of Fort Lauderdales most beloved beachfront restaurant, Bo's Beach, this hidden sanctuary calls out to travelers, adventurers, and wanderers alike.

 

Dance alongside the setting sun, immersed in lush foliage and the rhythmic vibrations of the worlds best international House Music DJs. With our breathtaking views of Fort Lauderdale Beach, this paradise found is a haven for the daring voyager inside us all. To learn more about La Playa Dayclub, please visit our official website.
