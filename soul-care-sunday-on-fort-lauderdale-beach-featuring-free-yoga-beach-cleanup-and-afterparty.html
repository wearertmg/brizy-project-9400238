Soul Care Sunday on Fort Lauderdale Beach - Featuring Free Yoga, Beach Cleanup & Afterparty

Food and Drinks at La Playa Dayclub

Photo by La Playa Dayclub Media Team

This Sunday, August 28th, La Playa Dayclub joins forces once again with Seagrass Clothing Company, a company focused on hands-on marine conservation efforts, and Lola Yoga, a full mind, body and soul wellness platform on a special Soul Care Sunday.


We kick off Soul Care Sunday with a free, 10 AM, all ages welcome, 1-hour yoga class to awaken the mind, body, and soul to the sounds of the sea. Guests should bring their own yoga mat or towel and will meet directly outside La Playa Dayclub located at 600 Seabreeze Blvd on Fort Lauderdale Beach.

Photo From Lola Yoga

“My intention with this yoga class is to open the hearts and minds of all, and to create a safe space where people feel free to express themselves,” says lead yoga instructor Nicole Samanez, better known in South Florida as thirdeyeyogiii. “I want our community and guests to come and feel the sand on their toes, the wind in their hair, and to connect together on a beautiful Sunday full of mindfulness, music and movement.”


Directly following Yoga by Lola, guests are welcome (all ages welcome) to join us in cleaning up our beach with the SeaGrass Clothing Company and the Coastline Vibes Crew. “It is important for us to come together as a community to protect our local environments,” says Seagrass Company Co-Founder, Christian Halliday. “We make it easy, we provide everything for you, just show up and show some love to one of our most beautiful ecosystems.”

Photo From Lola Yoga

The beach cleanup takes place from 11 am - 1 pm directly outside of La Playa Dayclub near the main Fort Lauderdale parking lot. Participants will sign in at the Seagrass tent where all materials including gloves, bags, and buckets will be provided. As a special thank you to those participating in our beach cleanup, La Playa Dayclub will offer all those 21+ free admissions for the day and a free refreshing beverage to unwind after a job well done.


“Our team enjoys partnering with young, motivated founders and companies to create unique experiences,” says Jason Shaw, Project Director of La Playa Dayclub and CEO of Round Table Marketing Group. “We really love the partnership with Lola and Seagrass and how this event is organically growing right here on our beach.”


For those who want to skip over the yoga and beach clean-up and head right to brunch, La Playa Dayclub will be open at 11 am and guests will be vibing to the funky house grooves of David Facada, White Rice and our resident DJ Mike Antix.

About La Playa Dayclub

Awaken your sense of discovery as you step into the intimate jungle oasis of La Playa Dayclub, Fort Lauderdale Beach’s first true luxury day club experience. Nestled on the 2nd floor of Fort Lauderdale’s most beloved beachfront restaurant, Bo's Beach, this hidden sanctuary calls out to travelers, adventurers, and wanderers alike.

 

Dance alongside the setting sun, immersed in lush foliage and the rhythmic vibrations of the world’s best international House Music DJs. With our breathtaking views of Fort Lauderdale Beach, this paradise found is a haven for the daring voyager inside us all. To learn more about La Playa Dayclub, please visit our official website.
