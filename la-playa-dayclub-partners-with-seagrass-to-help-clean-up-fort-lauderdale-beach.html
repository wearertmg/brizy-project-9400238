La Playa Dayclub Partners With Seagrass To Help Clean Up Fort Lauderdale Beach

La Playa Dayclub X Seagrass

We're Joining Forces To Clean Up Fort Lauderdale Beach!

This Sunday, June 12th at 11 am La Playa Dayclub will join forces with Seagrass Clothing Company to clean Fort Lauderdale Beach and have a little fun.  


We are really looking forward to this event, says Brando Garcia Manager of La Playa Dayclub.  Seagrass focuses on hands-on marine conservation efforts, right here in Fort Lauderdale, and with La Playa Dayclub just steps away from the sand, we must do our part to pitch in.


The beach cleanup will take place from 11 am -1 pm directly outside of La Playa Dayclub near the main Fort Lauderdale parking lot.   Participants will sign in at the Seagrass tent where all materials including gloves, bags, and buckets will be provided.


Seagrass Clothing Company Co-Founder, Christian Halliday, says I grew up here and recognize that this beach is a crucial part of our home and part of what makes Fort Lauderdale so special. We should all want to step up as members of the community and help take care of our waters. This beach clean-up is meant to provide an opportunity for us to come together, as a community, and take care of our home.


Those 21+ participants helping clean the beach this weekend will receive free admission into the new day club on Fort Lauderdale Beach, La Playa Dayclub, along with a free drink to cool off and unwind after a job well done.  


"We are beyond fortunate to be connected to such a beautiful place with so many amazing people. says La Playa Resident DJ Mike Antix, "Bringing our communities together and helping Seagrass with this conservation effort is one more thing we can do to ensure the preservation of our environment for us and for future generations. We want this type of effort to extend out to the adults in our community, as well as the youth who will be inheriting these shores."


For additional information on La Playa Dayclub, please visit - https://laplayadayclub.com/
