La Playa Dayclub Offers Free Admission To Locals

Sunsets At La Playa Dayclub

Photo by La Playa Dayclub Media Team

Fort Lauderdale, FL - This Labor Day Weekend, La Playa Dayclub invites you (21+) to come enjoy the club, the gorgeous views of Fort Lauderdale Beach and our vibes all for free. 

La Playa Dayclub, one of the fastest growing beach clubs in South Florida invites guests to awaken a sense of discovery as you step into the intimate jungle oasis of La Playa Dayclub, Fort Lauderdale Beach’s first true luxury day club experience. Opening in Spring 2022, this will be the first Labor Day Weekend Celebration for La Playa Dayclub, and they want to celebrate with a big local party encouraging anyone living in South Florida to sign up to the guest list and to enter the club with free admission. In addition, there will be $150 bottle specials for those who would like to enjoy some drinks at the day club.

“La Playa was built by our local community,” says Jason Shaw, CEO of Round Table Marketing and Partner at La Playa Dayclub. “We sourced local talent to help us from our early construction days.  We utilized local artists, staff, DJs, chefs, bartenders, photographers, videographers and more to help us launch La Playa and to help it build into the successful club you see today.  Our community is so important to us which is why on a major weekend like Labor Day we want to do something just for the locals.”

La Playa Dayclub is Nestled on the 2nd floor of Fort Lauderdale’s most beloved beachfront restaurant, Bo's Beach, and is a hidden open-air sanctuary with a retractable ceiling that allows guests to stay cool while enjoying the fun of the beach and the day club atmosphere.  It offers guests the opportunity to book VIP Cabanas for celebrations and to enjoy brunch or lunch in your own private space and a big dance floor to mix and mingle with friends.

Here is what they have planned:

La Playa Dayclub kicks off the big Labor Day Weekend celebration on Friday, September 2, 2022, with their popular “Island Vibes” reggae party, featuring South Florida’s very own DJ Impulse Disco and the award-winning number 1 reggae radio host in the US, Tanto Irie! Featuring our brand new extended Reggae Happy Hour starting at 5 PM, guests can enjoy $5 draft beers, $7 Tito's cocktails, $9 appetizers, and $150 bottle specials all while they sit back, relax and enjoy the island vibes.

On Saturday, September 3, 2022, and Sunday, September 4, 2022, everyone (21+) is invited to awaken a sense of discovery as you step into the intimate jungle oasis of La Playa Dayclub.  Dance to FTLs hottest house music DJs while enjoying gorgeous beach views and the cool ocean breeze in your hair. Enjoy a VIP cabana and featured bottles for only $150 dollars. A lunch and dinner menu is also available from 11 AM - 10 PM and reservations are required.

On Monday, September 5, 2022, La Playa Dayclub will be turning up the music and the heat from 3 PM -10 PM with our resident DJ, Mas Inc, curating the vibe. Guests will dance through the day to the sound of house music and the ocean breeze as they take advantage of our VIP cabanas and $150 bottle specials.

Guests must be 21+ to Drink, Dine and Dance at La Playa Dayclub.
