La Playa Dayclub Receives Recognition From Global Artists - This Weekend Featuring DJ RECAP!

Mas Inc, DJ RECAP, & Mike Antix

Photo by La Playa Dayclub Media Team

Fort Lauderdale Beach, FL - Get ready to dance under the stars! This Saturday, September 17, 2022, La Playa Dayclub welcomes DJ RECAP, a heavy-hitting house music artist all the way from London. Known by the South Florida DJ and producer communities as a collaboration advocate, DJ RECAP spent several months building partnerships with our talented locals, and resident vibe creators at La Playa Dayclub, some of whom will join him on stage this weekend.

Beginning in the 90s on vinyl, DJ RECAP lived through some of the most exciting times in dance music. From participating in the largest parties in the world, to performing across major European radio stations, his experience, network and music selection is unrivaled. Currently hosting a breakfast show on 103.7 FunkySx and performing for audiences across the globe, DJ RECAP has made music his life and now wants to share opportunities with others. “When I stumbled upon La Playa Dayclub earlier this year, I had no idea something like this existed in South Florida,” says DJ RECAP. I was blown away by the talented DJs and was amazed at the kindness and hospitality. We had engaging music conversations and deep connections were made, and in that moment, I knew I wanted to be involved. La Playa was definitely building something special.”

Featuring a full day of events right on the gorgeous South Florida coastline just steps away from Fort Lauderdale Beach. With brunch kicking off the day from 11 am - 3 pm and afternoon and evening programming beginning at 3 pm, guests are encouraged to come dine, drink, and dance on our second-floor open-air venue just steps from the beach. Other artists featured this day are La Playa’s very own vibe creators Mas Inc, Mike Antix and David Facada.

“We are so stoked to have DJ RECAP return to the states to play our stage,” says Joyce Mealey, Entertainment Manager at Round Table Marketing Group, the Entertainment Partner of La Playa Dayclub. “He is a very talented DJ with years of experience, which is something all of us can learn from. He has generously helped our artists gain exposure in the UK, and we appreciate that.”

For those who are not familiar with the sounds of DJ Recap, please listen to his latest mix, which hit 11th on the global disco charts and broke the top 30 on the global house music charts.

Admission to this event is FREE for anyone 21 and over who is on the guest list.  South Florida residents and music fans can sign up for our guest list HERE.

About La Playa Dayclub

Awaken your sense of discovery as you step into the intimate jungle oasis of La Playa Dayclub, Fort Lauderdale Beach’s first true luxury day club experience. Nestled on the 2nd floor of Fort Lauderdale’s most beloved beachfront restaurant, Bo's Beach, this hidden sanctuary calls out to travelers, adventurers, and wanderers alike.

 

Dance alongside the setting sun, immersed in lush foliage and the rhythmic vibrations of the world’s best international House Music DJs. With our breathtaking views of Fort Lauderdale Beach, this paradise found is a haven for the daring voyager inside us all. To learn more about La Playa Dayclub, please visit our official website.
