Celebrate Working Hard This Labor Day at La Playa Dayclub

Food and Drinks at La Playa Dayclub

Photo by La Playa Dayclub Media Team

Fort Lauderdale, FL - This Labor Day Weekend the hottest new beach club in Fort Lauderdale, La Playa Dayclub, invites you to play hard because we know you have been working hard. This year offers guests the opportunity to attend the debut Labor Day Weekend Party which includes four days of entertainment, a roster of incredibly talented DJs, $150 bottle specials and the best part? No cover for all locals who add their names to the guest list! La Playa Dayclub will be the place to be on Fort Lauderdale Beach. Relax and unwind as we drink, dance, and dine all weekend long! 


La Playa Dayclub kicks off the big Labor Day Weekend celebration on Friday, September 2, 2022, with their popular  Island Vibes reggae party, featuring South Floridas very own DJ Impulse Disco and the award-winning number 1 reggae radio host in the US, Tanto Irie! Featuring our brand new extended Reggae Happy Hour starting at 5 PM, guests can enjoy $5 draft beers, $7 Titos cocktails, $9 appetizers, and $150 bottle specials all while they sit back, relax and enjoy the island vibes.


On Saturday, September 3, 2022 and Sunday, September 4, 2022 everyone (21+) is invited to awaken a sense of discovery as you step into the intimate jungle oasis of La Playa Dayclub. Dance to FTLs hottest house music DJs while enjoying gorgeous beach views and the cool ocean breeze in your hair. Enjoy a VIP cabana and featured bottles for only $150 dollars. A lunch and dinner menu is also available from 11 AM - 10 PM and reservations are required.


On Monday, September 5, 2022, La Playa Dayclub will be turning up the music and the heat from 3 PM -10 PM with our resident DJ, Mas Inc, curating the vibe. Guests will dance through the day to the sound of house music and the ocean breeze as they take advantage of our VIP cabanas and $150 bottle specials.


We are excited about the celebration, says Jason Shaw, CEO of Round Table Marketing Group and Entertainment Partner at La Playa Dayclub. This will be our first Labor Day Celebration, and we plan to go big. We have great drink specials, the best DJs and a view that is simply unbeatable right on Fort Lauderdale Beach

Important Info

Guests must be 21+ to Drink, Dine and Dance at La Playa Dayclub.

About La Playa Dayclub

Awaken your sense of discovery as you step into the intimate jungle oasis of La Playa Dayclub, Fort Lauderdale Beachs first true luxury day club experience. Nestled on the 2nd floor of Fort Lauderdales most beloved beachfront restaurant, Bo's Beach, this hidden sanctuary calls out to travelers, adventurers, and wanderers alike.

 

Dance alongside the setting sun, immersed in lush foliage and the rhythmic vibrations of the worlds best international House Music DJs. With our breathtaking views of Fort Lauderdale Beach, this paradise found is a haven for the daring voyager inside us all. To learn more about La Playa Dayclub, please visit our official website.
